> how to remove extensions

✦ You can remove a Gemini CLI extension by using the gemini extensions
  uninstall command.

  To see a list of your installed extensions, you can run:
  gemini extensions list

  Once you know the name of the extension you want to remove, use the
  following command, replacing <extension-name> with the actual name:
  gemini extensions uninstall <extension-name>

  If you just want to temporarily disable an extension without removing it
  completely, you can use:
  gemini extensions disable <extension-name>
